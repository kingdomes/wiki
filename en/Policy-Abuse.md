# Abuse Policy

## Overview

AFNOOI-Network is committed to maintaining a secure, stable, and reliable network environment. This abuse policy is intended to clarify the procedures for handling network abuse and to protect the legitimate rights and interests of the DN42 community and other network participants.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to the following:

- Use of network resources provided by AFNOOI-Network (including but not limited to DNS services, domain hosting, tunnel nodes, etc.)
- Traffic originating from or transiting through the AFNOOI-Network
- Networks with a peering relationship with AFNOOI-Network

---

## Definition of Abuse

The following behaviors are considered network abuse and may trigger the procedures outlined in this policy:

| Category | Specific Behaviors |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Malicious Traffic** | DDoS/DoS attacks, port scanning, brute-force attempts, etc. |
| **Spam** | Unsolicited commercial email (UCE/UBE), SPAM, etc. |
| **Illegal Content** | Distribution of malware, phishing sites, copyright infringement, or other illegal content |
| **Network Interference** | IP spoofing, BGP route hijacking, DNS spoofing, etc. |
| **Resource Abuse** | Excessive DNS queries, bandwidth consumption, resource usage beyond reasonable limits, etc. |

## Handling Procedure

Upon receiving an abuse report, we will follow these steps:

1. **Acknowledgment**: Confirm receipt of the report within 24 hours.
2. **Initial Verification**: Review the provided evidence to determine whether the activity constitutes abuse as defined in this policy.
3. **Remedial Actions**: Depending on the severity, actions may include:
   - Sending a warning notice
   - Restricting relevant services or resources
   - Suspending or terminating network connectivity
   - Cooperating with the DN42 community or other network operators for joint action
4. **Feedback and Record**: Provide feedback to the reporter and retain records for future reference.
